# Roadmap

Strategic timeline for Zandbox initiatives across research, federal contracting, and platform development.

---

## Vision

**2024-2026:** Establish foundational capabilities across AGI research, federal contracting, and zero-budget AI systems.

**2026-2028:** Scale successful prototypes to production, publish academic contributions, secure sustainable contract vehicles.

**2028+:** Advance toward transformative AI capabilities with established revenue streams and validated methodologies.
